Biography




I’m now a graduate student in Pattern Recognition & Intelligent System Lab, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, supervised by Prof. Weihong Deng. Prior to that, I received my B.E from School of Information and Communication Engineering of BUPT in June 2013.

My research interests lie in face detection, blurred face recognition and deblurring.
